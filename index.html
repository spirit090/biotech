About Our Department

The Department of Biotechnology at Veeershaiva College was established with a vision to create a center of excellence in biotechnology education and research. Our BSc Biotechnology program offers a comprehensive curriculum that combines theoretical knowledge with practical skills.

We focus on providing students with a strong foundation in biological sciences, along with specialized knowledge in various biotechnology applications including genetic engineering, microbiology, biochemistry, and bioinformatics.

Our department is committed to nurturing innovative minds and creating skilled professionals who can contribute to the advancement of biotechnology in healthcare, agriculture, industry, and environmental conservation.
